US Brie

The non- detachable brie cheese does double duty as a USB port and a customized power bank connector.

Cacio Kindler

This plasma lighter is 100% sustainable, wind-proof, odorless, flameless, and 100% rechargeable. Use it for candles, incense or even a campfire.

Solar Milk

Connect the Solar Milk to any of the cheese modules to operate. The power bank features an on-off switch so you never have to worry about losing power when not in use.

Ricotta Torch

A flashlight or a mood light. The cheese module transforms into both when combined with the Solar Milk.

Swiss Note

This bluetooth speaker and radio cleverly hides its backlit LED buttons in “eyes” of the cheese. The bottom size of the speaker is connected to an auxiliary custom solar panel or hand crank magnetically.

Cheese Plate

A solar panel on one side, cheese plate on the other. The panel generates 5 volts at 1.2 amps — enough power to charge a smartphone as quickly as a wall charger.
